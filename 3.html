Webpage makeup is a creation of a hypertext document structure based on HTML markup with the use of stylesheets and client scripts so that design elements look similar to the layout.

HTML makeup differs from the printing one as it is necessary to take into account the difference of elements displayed depending on the browser and the devices" size difference.

The process is complex and has a creative basis. None of the methods is considered to be canonical. All approaches have their own benefits and drawbacks.
