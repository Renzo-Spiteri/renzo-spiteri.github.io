types of sandwiches

The most common type of sandwiches is a slice of bread or buns with some kind of snack stuffed in them (cheese, ham, sausage, caviar, meat, fish, canned food). They can be decorated with leafy and spicy vegetables, pieces of egg and lemon, olives, sauce, gherkin mayonnaise etc. The sandwiches are served on dishes, in vases with flat edges or on plates covered with napkins. As they are not subject to long-term storage, the sandwiches are cooked immediately before serving.

Hot sandwiches

Hot sandwiches are getting warmed up during preparation and consumed, as a rule, hot. Their filling is usually covered with a slice of cheese, and the sandwich is heated until the cheese melts. You can also use various types of herbs to decorate the sandwiches: parsley, lettuce, dill etc.
