When you are a crewmate

When you are the crew (crewmate) in among us you have to do tasks
and you have to vote the imposter out
As crew you have 2 buttons: Use and Report

List of easy tasks:

  • Download Data
  • Submit Scan
  • Oxygen Depleted In (sabotage)

When you are an imposter

When you are an imposter in Among Us you need to kill the crewmates or you vote them out
As imposter you have 3 buttons: kill sabotage and report

List of easy-win sabotages:

  • Fix Lights
  • Reactor Meltdown In

Hint: When lights are down you can kill and vent with less chance that the crewmates see you
